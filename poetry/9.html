  • I--I went to sleep and dreamed, I guess.
  • There was a chance that promised success.
  • Why should it not be made common and free?
  • 'I am,' saith he, 'like a green fir tree.
  • So I wander about in other rooms.
  • ' I inquired if he knew all their names.
  • "I don't want your money, I want my watch!
  • A hop and a scotch is another notch,
  • Goin' to turn him over to the sheriff?
  • Dost think thyself beyond the reach of mischief?
  • [Footnote 1: Without fear and without reproach.]
  • Miss Clara and me, we'll go by the coach.
  • But there Was Aunt Maude's noisy ring and knock!
  • Meanwhile, have hence this rebel to the block.
